White Cheese Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Noodles: Of course, you will need lasagna noodles.
  • Vegetables: You'll need an onion and 2 packages of frozen spinach.
  • Garlic: Cook a clove of minced garlic with the onion for a more intense flavor!
  • Butter: Cook the onion and garlic in butter
  • Flour: All-purpose flour thickens th sauce.
  • Broth: Use store-bought chicken broth, or make your own at home if you have the skills for it!
  • Milk: Whole milk is best for this decadent white sauce.
  • Seasonings and herbs: This chicken lasagna is seasoned with salt, dried basil, died oregano, black pepper, and fresh parsley.
  • Cheeses: You'll need mozzarella, Parmesan, and ricotta cheeses.
  • Chicken: This white chicken lasagna is a great use for leftover chicken!

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C)
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add lasagna noodles and cook for 8 to 10 minutes; drain and rinse with cold water.
  3. Meanwhile, melt butter in a large saucepan over medium heat; stir in onion and garlic. Whisk in flour until lightly browned and tender, about 2 minutes. Add chicken broth, milk, and salt; cook, while continuously whisking, for 1 minute.
  4. Stir in 2 cups mozzarella and 1/4 cup Parmesan until well combined. Season with basil, oregano, black pepper; remove from heat and set aside.
  5. Spread 1/3 of the sauce mixture in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Layer with 3 lasagna noodles, ricotta, and chicken. Arrange 3 lasagna noodles over the chicken and layer with 1/3 of the sauce mixture, spinach, remaining 2 cups mozzarella, and 1/2 cup of Parmesan. Arrange remaining noodles over cheese and spread remaining sauce evenly over noodles. Sprinkle with parsley and remaining 1/4 cup Parmesan.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes.
